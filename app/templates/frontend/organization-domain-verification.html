CertSecure Organization Domain Verification

Verifying domain name {{session.organization_domain_address}}

To continue the sign-up process, please add the following TXT record to the DNS settings of your domain {{session.organization_domain_address | lower}}. This step helps us to ensure that you are the owner of the domain.


Host Name: certsecure-domain-verification
Record Type: TXT
Time to Live (TTL): 3600
Value: {{domain_verification_token}}

Once you have added the TXT record to the DNS settings of your domain, please click on the button below to verify the domain.



Current Domain Verification Status: Not Verified


Please note that it may take up to 24 hours for the DNS changes to propagate, and for the domain to be verified. In case the domain is not verified within 24 hours, please contact support.

Note, that these records will need to be present in the DNS records of your domain, failing which you will be prompted to add the records again.
